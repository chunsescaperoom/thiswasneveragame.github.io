Taiwan title: 戰慄品 | Over My Dead Body
This is a physical immersive experience in Taiwan called 戰慄品. It was never designed to scale. It exists inside a very specific physical space. After three years in its current location, that space may no longer be available. If we cannot move it, this experience may disappear.

Project statement

Why now

Over My Dead Body is not a digital work. It cannot simply be copied or recreated instantly.

It exists as a physical environment — built piece by piece, dependent on space, structure, and timing.

After three years in its current location, the project is now facing a forced transition.

Moving it means dismantling, transporting, rebuilding, and keeping the experience alive long enough to reopen.

This is not about creating something new.
This is about moving something that already exists — before it disappears.
